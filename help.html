Basic page use

When you enter our website, you'll be redirected to the page that is your device's language. If the language is not correct, you can change it in the menu (read below about how to do it). If the website cannot detect the language, you'll have to select it manually.

At the top, you can see menu buttons, which on click, will put you in the selected place of the website or change the language.

Scrolling down, you can see the main page - sections "About us", "Shop" and "Contacts".

In "About us" section you can read about who we are.

In "Shop" section you can view and buy items (read in "Purchases" section, about how to do it).

In "Contacts" section you can view our contacts, cookies policy and rules of purchase (read in "Rules & terms" section, about how to do it), notify us about an error and track your order.

Purchases

You can buy items in our shop.

You can do it in the main page section "Shop", after selecting your item, clicking on it and clicking the button "Buy".

You can also buy items by clicking on the items, clicking the button "Read more" and clicking the button "Buy".

After clicking the button "Buy" you will be redirected to the payment. There, enter the data and click the confirmation button.

If everything is right, the payment will be completed.

NOTE: For now, we can only send items to Belgium and Lithuania, but are doing our best to add more coutries to ship to.

Prices everywhere in our page are written by Euros.

Price meanings: Eur - Euro (Euros), ct cent (cents).

NOTE: don't forget to click the button "I'm not a robot". If you don't click it, the confirmation button will not work.

Rules & terms

Our store has rules of purchase and cookies policy.

You can read the rules of purchase at the bottom of the main page, after clicking the button "Rules of purchase".

You can read the cookies policy at the bottom of the main page, after clicking the button "Cookies policy".

Cookies

Our website uses cookies.

After entering the website you will see a "Cookies Consent" box on your screen. You can agree, or learn more about the cookies.

What are cookies, what they're used for, and other info about the cookies, you can read after clicking the button "Learn more".
